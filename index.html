Canada COVID-19 Vaccination Schedule

These charts show counts of vaccine doses administered to-date as reported by provinces (blue bars) and a calculated count of dose administration required to fully vaccinate age 15+ population by Sep 30, 2021 (gray bars). Canada's government has stated a goal of providing vaccinations for all age 15+ Canadians by Sep 30, 2021.

The calculated count of dose administration required to meet the goal is simply the population x 2 minus the count of doses administered to-date. Daily dose counts required to meet the goal is simply the calculated count of dose administration divided by the number of days between the last actual reporting date and Sep 30, 2021.

Population x 2 is used because reporting is by dose and two doses are required for full vaccination using the available Pfizer and Moderna vaccines. This would be updated if and when single dose vaccines become available.

COVID-91 vaccine data comes from the COVID-19 Canada Open Data Working Group who are doing an amazing job collating provincial COVID-19 data!
